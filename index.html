Our Evolution

1st Season - The Beginning

2022-2023

Our journey began with four dedicated founding members: Brian, Wei Sheng, Yee Keat, and Boon Hee. Starting from scratch, we learned the fundamentals of FTC robotics, built our first robot, and established the foundation of Team Absolute Hack.

Team Formation First Robot Build Competition Debut

2nd Season - Growth & Excellence

2023-2024

Expanding our team with five talented new members: Xing Ern, Kai Xin, Xin Bing, Phee Jun, and Caryn. This season marked our evolution into a well-rounded team with specialized roles in programming, mechanical design, CAD, finance, and outreach.

Inspire Award Innovate Award Alliance Captains Team Expansion

Current Season - Innovation Continues

2024-2025

Building on our success, we continue to push the boundaries of innovation, focusing on advanced programming techniques, precision mechanical design, and community outreach. Our goal is to maintain our winning streak while inspiring the next generation of robotics enthusiasts.

Advanced Programming Enhanced CAD Design Community Impact
